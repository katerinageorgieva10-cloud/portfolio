Behind the Pen

Hi, I'm Katerina.

I've been holding a pen for as long as I can remember. That early obsession led to my first exhibitions and a national award while I was still a child, and eventually to studying at the National School of Arts in Bulgaria.

My foundation is in classical fine arts, with a specialty in sculpture — but my heart has always belonged to graphic work and expressive, high-contrast illustration. That traditional training is the backbone of everything I create, even when the final piece feels modern, soft, or playful.

I don't simply copy photos. Whether I'm working on a mural, a window display, a tattoo design, or a custom portrait, I reinterpret what I see through my own visual language. I aim to capture the soul of a person or a pet — stylized, expressive, never flat.

For me, this isn't just about creating something pretty. The real magic happens when someone feels something looking at their piece. That connection is what matters.

If my style resonates with you, I'd love to create something meaningful together.
